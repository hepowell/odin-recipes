Plab Patty

Description

Before the Krabby Patty came The Plab Patty. Created by Mr. Krabbs and Plankton it was their first and last creation. The first one almost killed someone but we think you'll be fine...

Ingredients

  • 1 Bun
  • 1 Krabby Patty
  • Lettuce
  • Onions
  • Pickles
  • Tomatoes
  • Ketchup
  • Mustard
  • Cheese
  • Tomatoes

Steps

  1. Shine the bun
  2. We suggest buying frozen pattys but if you insist on making from scratch you'll need: seaweed, squid ink and a pinch of chum (omit if you're afraid of potential poisoning)
  3. Grill the patty
  4. Assemble
  5. Order matters, assemble as following:
    • Bottom Bun
    • Patty
    • Lettuce
    • Cheese
    • Onions
    • Tomatoes
    • Ketchup
    • Mustard
    • Pickles
    • Top Bun
  6. Enjoy
